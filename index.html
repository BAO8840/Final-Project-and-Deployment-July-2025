Our Services

We provide practical services for farmers, cooperatives and agribusinesses. Below are our main offerings.

Field Training & Demonstrations

On-site extension training covering planting, spacing, integrated pest management, and post-harvest handling.

Market Linkages & Aggregation

We connect producers to buyers, help form aggregators/cooperatives, and support contract arrangements.

Agritech Integration

Digital tools for record-keeping, weather alerts, market prices, and farm management for smallholders.

Business Advisory & Finance

Business planning, access to grants/loans, input bundling and investor introductions.
